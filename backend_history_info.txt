BACKEND HISTORY SECTION
=======================

YES, there is a dedicated history section in the backend.

1. Endpoint
   --------
   GET /api/admin/history

2. Controller
   ----------
   File: src/main/java/com/vms/backend/controller/ApprovalController.java
   Method: getHistory()

3. Functionality
   -------------
   - It fetches ALL records from the `approval` table.
   - It sorts them by `requestDate` in descending order (newest first).
   - This serves as the "History" or "Reports" data source for the frontend.
